HOW TO USE
WHAT THIS APP DOES
Analyses live Boom & Crash prices every 2 seconds and tells you the best moment to BUY, SELL or WAIT. Uses SMC market structure, EMA alignment, momentum, spike probability and post-spike entry techniques.
READING THE MAIN SIGNAL
▲BUY — Bullish structure. For Boom: trend heading toward next upward spike. For Crash: price bouncing after crash, follow the rally.
▼SELL — Bearish structure. For Crash: trend dropping toward next crash spike. For Boom: pulling back after boom spike.
◈WAIT — Mixed signals. No edge. Stay out until signal clarifies.
SPIKE ZONE — MOST IMPORTANT NUMBER
1EARLY — Spike just happened. Trade the trend between spikes.
2BUILDING — Probability growing. Stay alert.
3WARM — Spike becoming likely. Tighten SL if in a trade.
4HOT (80%+) — Spike overdue. Strongest signal. Look to enter NOW.
HOW TO ENTER A TRADE
1Wait for BUY or SELL signal with confidence above 60%
2Check Spike Zone — HOT adds strongest weight to signal
3Confluences tab — need 3+ factors on one side
4Open MT5 → place trade using the SL and TP levels shown
5Hit TP1 → move SL to entry → let trade run to TP2/TP3
POST-SPIKE ENTRIES (HIGHEST PROBABILITY)
▲After BOOM spike UP: Wait 3–10 tick pullback → Enter BUY on first bullish tick → SL below pullback low
▼After CRASH spike DOWN: Wait 3–15 tick bounce → Enter SELL on first bearish tick → SL above bounce high
RISK RULES
⚠Max 1% of account per trade. Never risk more than you can afford to lose.
⚠Never enter when signal says WAIT. Stop after 3 losses in a row.
